about me

Hello! My name is Iris Westerman. I have been an artist for twelve years. Throughout my life, I have used my art to decompress, to express myself, to explore cultures, and to help others. More recently, I have been able to experiment with different mediums such as printmaking and bookbinding. I intend to continue to work hard to improve my skills and stay dedicated to both my scientific ambitions and my artistic inclination.
